eKnow

Oblique Solutions, co-created by Brian Eno, is a card set with vague but helpful advice designed to help musicians overcome challenges during writing and recording. Leveraging the oblique solutions API, I built an app that pairs text from a random card with random imagery, and allows the user to muse on how the advice applies to their own situation. It offers users a chance to have a one-way conversation with yourself to find perspective, a little like rubber-ducky coding.
